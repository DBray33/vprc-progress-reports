**Problem:** `/gutter-maintenance-installation` generated 30,734 impressions in May 2026 but only 2 clicks — a CTR of 0.0065% — making it the single most wasteful page on the site by impression-to-click ratio, while "gutter repair" (6,235 impressions, position 36.2) and "gutter replacement" (2,278 impressions, position 48.1) are both ranking too deep to earn clicks.

**Action:** 

**Impact:** Even a modest CTR improvement from 0.0065% to 0.5% on the existing 30,734 impressions would generate roughly 150 additional clicks per month from that page alone. Ranking improvements for "gutter repair" and "gutter replacement" are a secondary benefit over a 60–90 day horizon as the page gains relevance signals.

**Priority:** High

## 3. Resolve the Homepage Duplicate Impression Split Diluting Authority

**Problem:** The HTTPS homepage (`https://valleypeakroof.com/`) and the HTTP/www variant (`http://www.valleypeakroof.com/`) are appearing as separate entries in Search Console, collectively accumulating 54,216 impressions and 68 clicks — meaning a significant share of the site's most valuable impressions are split across a URL that should not be indexable at all.

**Action:** 

**Impact:** Consolidating impression and authority signals from the duplicate variant into the canonical homepage should improve the homepage's effective ranking signals and reduce Search Console noise within 30–45 days of Google recrawling. The 19,527 impressions currently attributed to the non-canonical URL represent authority that belongs on the primary domain.

**Priority:** High
