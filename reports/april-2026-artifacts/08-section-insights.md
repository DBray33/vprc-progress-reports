# Section Insights

## ga4

Engagement rate climbed <strong>+4.2pp MoM to 46.6%</strong> even as sessions dipped -1.1% — a signal that the audience shrinking is actually a quality filter, not a loss. The traffic that remains is more engaged, which matters more for a service business than raw volume.

## trafficSources

Direct traffic dominates at <strong>388 of 789 sessions (49%)</strong> but carries the weakest engagement rate at <strong>35.6%</strong>, while organic search delivers only 257 sessions yet converts attention at <strong>59.5%</strong> — nearly 24 points higher. Organic is the highest-quality channel by a wide margin; the -23.7% MoM drop in organic sessions is the most urgent number in this report.

## topPages

The homepage is carrying the entire click load at <strong>88 of 166 total clicks (53%)</strong>, which is a concentration risk — one ranking shift and traffic craters. The `/roof-financing-options` page is a standout opportunity: <strong>2,298 impressions at position 13.8</strong> with only 4 clicks, meaning a push to page one could unlock significant volume from an already-warm audience.

## localPack

<strong>9 Local Pack wins</strong> are active, representing a meaningful foothold in the map results where roofing leads convert at the highest rate. The `/service-areas/roofing-emmaus-pa` page at <strong>position 12.2 with 701 impressions</strong> signals Google is testing local area pages — expanding this template to additional service towns is the clearest near-term Local Pack growth lever.

## keywords

<strong>20 problem keywords</strong> are generating high impressions but low clicks, and with overall CTR at just <strong>0.19%</strong> against 87,797 impressions, there's a large pool of visibility that isn't converting to traffic. These are the highest-leverage targets for title tag and meta description rewrites — the impressions are already there, the click hasn't been earned yet.

## competitors

<strong>alankunsmanroofing.com</strong> is the primary threat with <strong>38 keywords in common</strong> — the most overlap of any competitor — while homeshieldpros.com holds <strong>148 total ranked keywords</strong> versus Valley Peak's 347, suggesting different keyword territory rather than a direct head-to-head. The gap to watch: homeshieldpros.com's broader keyword footprint at 148 total indicates they may be building topical authority in areas Valley Peak hasn't yet targeted.
