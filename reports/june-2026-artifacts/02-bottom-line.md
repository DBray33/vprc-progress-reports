Next month, the priority is clear: recover the lost transactional keywords by strengthening dedicated service-plus-location pages, and push the AI visibility score beyond its current <strong style="color: #FFD60A;">28% mention rate</strong> by deepening topical authority content before the late-summer storm season peaks.</p>
