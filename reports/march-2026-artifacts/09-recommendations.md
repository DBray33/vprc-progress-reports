**Action:** 

**Impact:** Moving "gutter repair near me" from position 34 to the low 20s within 60–90 days is realistic with dedicated page creation and internal linking. Even a position-20 ranking on that keyword at a 1–2% CTR would yield 30–60 additional clicks per month from that single keyword. Combined with the other four gutter terms, this is the single largest untapped click pool in the current keyword portfolio.

**Priority:** High

**Action:** 

**Impact:** AI referral leads are currently at zero. Expect Gemini URL citations to appear within 60–90 days of publishing structured, authoritative content.
