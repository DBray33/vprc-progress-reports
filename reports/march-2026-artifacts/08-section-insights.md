# Section Insights

## ga4

Total sessions dropped <strong>-59.2% MoM to 798</strong>, but engagement rate jumped <strong>+16.0pp to 42.5%</strong> — the traffic that remained was meaningfully more qualified, suggesting the March dip reflects a loss of low-intent visitors (likely direct/branded) rather than an SEO collapse.

## trafficSources

Direct traffic (394 sessions) is outpacing Organic Search (337 sessions) but converts poorly, with a <strong>27.4% engagement rate vs. 62.0% for organic</strong> — organic visitors are more than twice as likely to engage, making it the highest-quality channel despite being the smaller one by volume.

## topPages

The homepage dominates with <strong>111 clicks at position 2.9</strong>, but `/gutter-maintenance-installation` is the biggest missed opportunity: <strong>19,499 impressions with only 5 clicks</strong> at position 43 — a page sitting on a massive audience it can't reach yet.

## localPack

Valley Peak holds <strong>9 Local Pack wins</strong> across 3,746 total keywords — a foothold worth protecting and expanding, especially heading into spring storm season when "roof inspection Easton" and "storm damage repair" queries spike and map pack clicks capture the majority of local intent.

## keywords

With <strong>20 problem keywords</strong> flagged for high impressions and low clicks, and overall CTR at just <strong>0.29%</strong> against 83,032 impressions, there's a significant volume of Google exposure that isn't converting to traffic — title tag and meta description rewrites on those pages are the fastest lever to pull.

## competitors

<strong>alankunsmanroofing.com</strong> is the primary threat with 39 keywords in common — nearly double the overlap of the next closest competitor — while <strong>homeshieldpros.com</strong> ranks for 147 total keywords vs. Valley Peak's 356, suggesting a narrower but potentially more targeted competitor worth monitoring for content gaps.
